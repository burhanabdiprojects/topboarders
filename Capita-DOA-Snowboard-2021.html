The 2021 Capital D.O.A. (Defenders of Awesome) series has earned itself an enviable reputation as being one of the best all-terrain freestyle boards on the market. With an unprecedented seven consecutive Transworld Good Wood’s to its name, it has rewritten the rule book for performance snowboards. Aimed at strong intermediates through to pro-level riders, its combination of state of the art construction, versatile twin chassis and Capita’s ride anything Resort V1 camber profile deliver a remarkable ride over the whole mountain. Equally, at home lapping the park as it is exploring the backcountry or charging around the resort, the Defenders of Awesome is the ultimate expression of the term a single quiver board. So if you’re after a high performing board, that’s going to let you ride the whole mountain and never feel wanting, you’re not going to get much better than the D.O.A.
